Why Choose Us

At Prime Pools we offer a range of services tailored around your needs. From a one of clean to a regular planned maintenance & valet to your pool. Our maintenance plan involves monitoring the chemicals in the swimming pool are kept in optimal range, cleaning the pool (brush and vacuum), inspection, checking the performances of the pumps and filtration and periodically cleaning of covers

For our regular maintenance clients you can feel at ease knowing your pool is in good hands and for your peace of mind we have 24 hour emergency contact number.
